Do I "Shape-Up"?

Problem

Me! Hi, I'm the problem it's me!
I refuse to believe it, surely not.
Most junior roles look for grads with 3+ years experience, youngsters that are raring to go and get stuck into their forever 9-5 ASAP careers.

That's not me. I am not a grad, and I'm very much not a junior (80's kid alert!)...

I'm a curious, life-long learner, attempting a career switch after falling for coding after embarking on a new hobby.
Shaped by the first tastes of online socialising and coping with dial up, feeling like a hacker with limewire and the sounds from Greenhill Zone permanently etched in my brain, growing up in the 90's made me who I am today. A career-switcher, a curious soul who has lived a few lives already — across countries and careers — and everything led me here. So yeah, maybe I’m the “problem” the hiring filters don't usually favour.

But 37Signals is different.

Appetite

I'm in this for the long haul, for the opportunities not only to learn, grow and to feed my personal sense of accomplishment but to be part of a team that is building something great.

Away from corporate fluff, office drama and disconnected leadership.
